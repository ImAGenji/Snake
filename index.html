Hero's Journey Archetype

Stage 1

Exposition 1. Call to Adventure: Hero introduced, normal life disrupted, the hero faces a life changing problem.

Rising Action 2. Refusal of the Call: The hero does not want to accept change, the hero doubts personal ability to cope.

Rising Action 3. Beginning of the Adventure: The hero accepts the challenge and begins an emotional, spiritual journey.

Stage 2

Rising Action 4. Road of Trials: Hero faces a series of challenges.

Rising Action 5. Experience with Unconditional Love: Love drives the hero on to continue their journey/quest.

Climax 6. Ultimate Boon: The hero gains what they set out to do.
